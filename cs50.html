HavardX CS50x (Soon Available)

Right now, I am enrolled in several of HavardX's CS50 classes. HardvardX provides recordings of a variety of Harvard courses online via the edX learning platform.

The first course, CS50x, deals with good programming practices, runtime optimization, data structures, web programming and SQL, thereby helping to circumvent dangerous half-knowledge. As Data Scientists, we are often well trained in econometrics, but know little about our everyday tools for analyzing data. In an effort to better understand underlying programming fundamentals, I have therefore taken a step back, delving into the deep-rooted basics of computer science and the programming in C. To complete CS50x students are required to submit 10 problem sets, 10 labs, and a final project. The final project will be made available here upon completion

The second course, CS50AI , provides an introduction to artificial intelligence using Python. Twelve problems and seven quizzes must be submitted in order to complete this course. Topics covered include: graph search algorithms; adversarial search; knowledge representation; logical inference; probability theory; Bayesian networks; Markov models; constraint satisfaction; machine learning; reinforcement learning; neural networks and natural language processing.
